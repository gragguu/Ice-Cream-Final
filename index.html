About Us

Shantala Ice Creams was started by a loving Grandpa and Grandma who shared a deep passion for ice creams. With just a small fridge and big dreams, they began their journey serving delicious flavours to their neighbourhood. Today, their legacy is carried forward by their son and grandson, offering a wide variety of creative and tasty ice creams that bring joy to every bite.
